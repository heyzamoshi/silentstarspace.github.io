Art born from stillness.

The Sanctuary is a living sculpture designed to hold space for pause, reflection, and transformation.

The form opens like a desert flower: protective from the outside, luminous within. Its ribs, thresholds, and warm interior light invite visitors to slow down, step in, and meet the silence at the center of the work.
